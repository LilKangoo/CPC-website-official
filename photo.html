1. Receive the image - You get an image from the previous player. Only you can see this image until the game ends. VERY IMPORTANT DON'T SEND ANY IMAGE FROM THE GAME TO ANYONE!
  2. Identify the theme  - Look at the image you received and think what the previous person had in mind. Don’t ask the person. Let your imagination and creativity take over! When you identified the theme check your archives and get an image you chose ready to send.
  3. Send your image to next player - You send your image for the collective to the next player. Only you and the next player can see this image until the game ends. VERY IMPORTANT DON'T SEND ANY IMAGE FROM THE GAME TO ANYONE!
